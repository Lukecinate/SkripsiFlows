# Decision Records

## ADR-001: Product name
- Decision: SkripsiFlow.
- Rationale: clear Indonesian audience signal, memorable, and compatible with future workflow features.

## ADR-002: Parsing strategy
- Decision: deterministic-first hybrid.
- Rationale: minimizes silent errors and preserves traceability; AI assists only ambiguity.

## ADR-003: Session model
- Decision: no account for initial release with temporary retention.
- Rationale: lowers onboarding friction for students while keeping privacy boundaries explicit.

## ADR-004: Editor interaction
- Decision: drag-and-drop structural blocks, not individual text fragments.
- Rationale: improves organization while reducing accidental content corruption.
